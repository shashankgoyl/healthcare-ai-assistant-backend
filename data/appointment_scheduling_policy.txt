=== APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING GUIDELINES ===

OVERVIEW:
Our scheduling policy ensures patients receive timely, appropriate care while optimizing provider availability across all departments.

SCHEDULING METHODS:
1. Online Portal: Available 24/7 at patient portal website
2. Phone: Call scheduling department during business hours (Mon-Fri, 8AM-6PM)
3. Mobile App: Book appointments through our official app
4. In-Person: Visit registration desk at any facility location
5. Telehealth: Available for qualifying appointments through the online portal

APPOINTMENT TYPES AND AVAILABILITY:
- Routine Check-ups: 2-4 weeks advance scheduling recommended
- Specialist Consultations: 1-6 weeks depending on specialty and urgency
- Urgent Care Appointments: Same-day or next-day availability
- Annual Wellness Exams: Schedule up to 6 months in advance
- Follow-up Appointments: Scheduled before leaving the facility or within 48 hours post-discharge
- Telehealth Appointments: Available 7 days per week, 7AM-9PM

DEPARTMENTS AND SCHEDULING WINDOWS:
- Primary Care: Monday through Friday, 8AM-5PM; Saturday 9AM-12PM
- Cardiology: Monday, Wednesday, Friday 9AM-4PM
- Orthopedics: Tuesday, Thursday 8AM-3PM
- Dermatology: Monday through Thursday 10AM-4PM
- Neurology: Wednesday and Friday 9AM-3PM
- Pediatrics: Monday through Friday 8AM-6PM; Saturday 9AM-1PM
- OB/GYN: Monday through Friday 9AM-5PM
- Mental Health: Monday through Friday 8AM-7PM; Saturday 9AM-2PM
- Oncology: By referral only; Monday through Thursday
- Radiology/Imaging: Daily 7AM-8PM; walk-ins accepted for certain tests

CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the appointment.
- Late cancellations (less than 24 hours notice) may incur a fee.
- No-show appointments may result in a $50 administrative fee.
- Three no-shows within 12 months may result in scheduling restrictions.
- Emergency cancellations due to illness or family emergencies are exempt from fees with documentation.

RESCHEDULING:
- Rescheduling requests should be made at least 24 hours in advance.
- Patients can reschedule online, by phone, or through the mobile app.
- Priority rescheduling is given to patients with urgent medical needs.

NEW PATIENT APPOINTMENTS:
- New patients must complete registration forms 48 hours before their appointment.
- Insurance verification is required prior to scheduling for non-emergency visits.
- New patients should arrive 20 minutes early to complete paperwork.
- Photo ID and insurance card are required at check-in.

INSURANCE AND PAYMENT:
- Insurance eligibility is verified at the time of scheduling.
- Co-pays are due at time of service.
- Patients without insurance should inquire about self-pay options and payment plans.
- Pre-authorization may be required for specialist visits; allow 3-5 business days.

SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS:
- Wheelchair accessible appointments available at all locations.
- Language interpreters available with 48-hour notice.
- Home visit appointments available for qualifying patients with mobility limitations.
- Priority appointments available for patients with urgent medical needs.

APPOINTMENT REMINDERS:
- Automated reminders sent via SMS, email, or phone call 48 hours before appointments.
- Patients can confirm, cancel, or reschedule via reminder links.
- Additional reminders sent 2 hours before appointment.

WAIT TIME POLICY:
- Patients who arrive more than 15 minutes late may be asked to reschedule.
- Average wait time for scheduled appointments is 10-15 minutes.
- Walk-in patients are seen in order of arrival and medical urgency.

PEDIATRIC APPOINTMENTS:
- Parents or legal guardians must accompany minors under 18.
- Bring immunization records to all pediatric visits.
- After-hours pediatric nurse line available for urgent questions.

REFERRAL PROCESS:
- Primary care provider referrals required for most specialty appointments.
- Referrals valid for 90 days from issue date.
- Self-referrals accepted for dermatology, gynecology, and mental health.
